Introduction

The main purpose of this website is to allow for those who wish to learn more about myth and folklore to be able to more easily start their journey. We plan on doing this in two ways one, summarizing some myths and legends into a more easily understood this way you can learn a bit about a couple of myths and decide whether or not you would be interested in learning more. The second way we try to help out newcomers or even people a little more seasoned is by providing our users with resources so that they can further their understanding on their own time.
